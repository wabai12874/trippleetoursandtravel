Who we are.

Welcome to Tripple-E Tours and Travels. We are a young and dynamic, fast growing tours and travel company in Kenya.
One of our greatest aims is delivering high quality travelling services to individual travellers, groups, cooperates and even institutions of learning at competitive prices.
We offer access to high quality travel infrastructure across the country at amazing prices.
Our services include;

  • Access to the best hotels
  • Accomodation Arrangements.(Individuals and groups)
  • Wild Animals Tours and Game Drives
  • Experience Amazing Cultures
  • Foreign Students Group Trips and Arrangements
